ABOUT VBS 2026

Three Days of
Adventure & Faith!

Vacation Bible School at LEF Church Magatapalli is a fun-filled, faith-building experience for children of all ages! Over three amazing days, kids will learn Bible stories, sing uplifting songs, make new friends, and discover the love of God in exciting ways.

Whether your child is coming for the first time or returning, VBS 2026 will be unforgettable. All are welcome! 🎉
