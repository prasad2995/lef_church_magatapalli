About Us

A Place of Faith,
Hope & Community

Welcome to LEF Church, Magatapalli — a vibrant community of believers united in faith, love, and the Word of God. We are part of the Living Empowered Fellowship, committed to worshipping God with all our heart.

Whether you are visiting for the first time or have been with us for years, you are cherished here. Come as you are — God's grace is for everyone.
